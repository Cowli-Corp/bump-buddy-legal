Privacy Policy

Bump Buddy — Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

Bump Buddy is a free, private, offline pregnancy companion app. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers — because we have no servers. Everything stays on your device.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. Bump Buddy does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any third-party service that accesses your data.

Data stored on your device

The app stores the following information locally on your device using your phone's built-in storage:

  • Your due date or last menstrual period date
  • Journal entries you write
  • Mood check-ins
  • Checklist progress
  • App preferences (perspective, notification settings)

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to our systems, shared with third parties, or accessible to us in any way.

Notifications

If you opt in to weekly reminders, these are scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to any server to deliver them.

Medical information

The medical reference content in this app is for general educational purposes only. It was not written or reviewed by licensed medical professionals and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider.

Children

This app is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Data deletion

To delete all your data, uninstall the app.

Changes to this policy

If we add features that change how data is handled, we will update this policy and the "last updated" date above.
